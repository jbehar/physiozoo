Overview

Fig. Examplary simulated signal.

The PHYSIOZOO project is a reference open-source platform for heart rate variability analysis research from mammals electrophysiological signals.

Features

Heart rate

Interface for heart rate evaluation and manual correction.

Different mamals

Heart rate variability analysis for different mamals.

Open-source algorithms

Algorithms for processing RR time series ( see documentation) .

Large Database

large freely available database ECG signals from different mamal species- see PHYSIOZOODB.

Examples

numerous examples on using the toolbox.

Authors

FECGSYN is the product of a collaboration between the Department of Engineering Science, University of Oxford (DES-OX), the Institute of Biomedical Engineering, TU Dresden (IBMT-TUD) and the Biomedical Engineering Faculty at the Technion Israel Institute of Technology (BME-IIT). The authors are:

  • Joachim Behar (DES-OX,BME-IIT)
  • Fernando Andreotti (DES-OX,IBMT-TUD)
  • Julien Oster (DES-OX)
  • Sebastian Zaunseder (IBMT-TUD)
  • Gari D. Clifford (DES-OX)

The main goal is to advance research in NI-FECG analysis by creating a collaborative platform for sharing algorithms, simulators and data.
